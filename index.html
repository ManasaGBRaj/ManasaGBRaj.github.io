Selected Projects

  • Electronic & Optical Properties of Perovskites
    DFT and Phonopy-based study of stability, bandgap tuning, and optoelectronic behaviour of double perovskites.
  • Band Alignment of 2D/3D Heterostructures
    Investigated charge redistribution, work function, and interface physics in layered vdW heterostructures.
  • AIMD Simulations of Functional Materials
    Studied finite-temperature behavior, dynamic stability, and structural response under thermal conditions.
